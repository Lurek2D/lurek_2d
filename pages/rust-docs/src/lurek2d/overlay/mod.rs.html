1//! Screen-space overlay sub-system: ambient lighting, atmospheric effects, and scene transitions.
2//!
3//! - `ambient` — ambient colour driven by time-of-day for scene-wide lighting tint.
4//! - `atmosphere` — atmospheric overlays: clouds, fog layers, and lightning flashes.
5//! - `controller` — top-level overlay scheduler for weather, fades, haze, and screen burns.
6//! - `screen_effects` — short-lived screen-space flash, shake, and fade state.
7//! - `transition` — full-screen scene transition effects with configurable easing.
8//! - `water` — animated water distortion overlay for underwater and rain scenarios.
9//! - `weather` — particle-based weather simulation: rain, snow, hail, dust, leaves, and ash.
10
