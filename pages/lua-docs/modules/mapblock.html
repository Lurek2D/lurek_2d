lurek.mapblock

`lurek.mapblock` — Block-based map assembly system. - Registers `lurek.mapblock.*` functions and types via `register()`. - Userdata types: `LuaMapBlockConfig`, `LuaMapBlock`, `LuaMapGroup`. - Userdata types: `LuaMapScript`, `LuaNeighborRules`, `LuaPlacementGrid`. - Userdata types: `LuaMapBlockGenerator`, `LuaMapBlockResult`, `LuaTilesetRef`.
